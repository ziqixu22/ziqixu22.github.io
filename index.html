    Ziqi Xu, Prajeet Basu, Zifan Dong , Adam Wawrowski, Qingyu Yi, Yuliy Baryshnikov, Haoyuan Li, Anji Dong, Ning Jiang

    Our research focused on analyzing datasets of academic publications from the math faculty, including citations, journals, MSC codes, and references. Among the clustering methods explored, K-means on the MSC dataset provided the most coherent clusters, highlighting its effectiveness in categorizing research areas. Additionally, we constructed phylogenetic trees using agglomerative clustering to identify the median or barycenter, utilizing iterative operations. This work outlines the processes involved and suggests areas for future enhancement.
